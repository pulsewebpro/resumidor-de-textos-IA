Simplify · Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Simplify (“we”, “us”) handles personal data when you use the Service.

1. Data controller

PULSE WEB PRO is responsible for the Service. Contact privacy@simplify.ai for privacy questions.

2. Data we process

  • Usage identifiers: we generate an anonymous user ID stored locally in your browser to keep track of available credits.
  • Input text: prompts are sent to the AI model transiently to generate the requested output. We do not persist user text.
  • Payment data: handled exclusively by Stripe. We receive payment status, plan type and metadata but never card numbers.

3. Cookies and storage

We use localStorage for the anonymous wallet token and language preferences. No marketing cookies are set.

4. Legal basis

Processing is based on performance of a contract (providing the Service) and legitimate interests (security and fraud prevention).

5. Data retention

Wallet tokens expire automatically according to the plan duration. AI prompts are not retained after the response is delivered. Payment records are kept according to accounting obligations.

6. Data sharing

We share limited data with trusted processors: Stripe (payments) and OpenAI (AI inference). Each processor acts under contract and cannot use your data for their own purposes.

7. International transfers

Processors may operate outside your jurisdiction. Appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses are in place when required.

8. Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion or restriction of your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Updates

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in the Service or regulation. We will notify users through the product interface when significant changes occur.
