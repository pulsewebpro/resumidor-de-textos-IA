Simplify · Cookies & Local Storage

Simplify uses minimal browser storage to operate the wallet and language preferences. No advertising cookies are set.

Essential storage

  • simplify_uid: anonymous identifier used to assign free credits and link purchases. Stored in localStorage.
  • simplify_set: secure token containing the remaining credits and plan. Stored in localStorage to authenticate API calls.
  • simplify_lang: language preference for the interface.
  • simplify_admin: optional flag to activate local admin mode for QA.

You can clear these values at any time via your browser settings. Deleting them will reset the wallet and require a new free allocation.

Third-party cookies

Stripe Checkout may set its own cookies when you proceed to payment. These cookies are controlled by Stripe to secure the checkout experience.

For further information contact privacy@simplify.ai.
